Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I submit more than 1 picture and win more than 1 prize?

You are welcomed to enter as many pictures as you wish! However, each individual is only entitled to win one prize.

2. Can I edit my pictures?

Yes

3. How will the judges pick the winners?

The judges will handpick the winners based on the clearest picture along with the most creative caption.

4. Can I submit my photo via Instagram/Twitter/Facebook?

Yes. But you will need to set your Instagram/Twitter/Facebook account in Public mode (not Private) during the entire contest period from 14th February 2015 to 1st March 2015 (23:59), until the announcement of winners to maintain eligibility in the contest.

5. Can I add other tags for my submission?

To be eligible, you’re required to include hashtag of the contest name (#minioreocar) in your photo or caption. You can also add in other hashtag to your liking.

6. Must the picture of the car taken while it is stationary or on the road?

No. As long as you spot the Mini Oreo car, you can snap a picture of it.

7. Do I need to be part of the picture too?

No, but if you wish to do so, there are no penalties.

8. Are there limitations to the language of the submission or length of the caption?

The caption of every submission is strictly to be in the English language.
The brand has no restrictions on the length of your caption. However, you will be limited to the social media platform you post in; for example, you are confined to a maximum of 140 characters via Twitter.

9. Will the number of likes for my post on Instagram, Twitter and Facebook add to my possibilities of winning?

No, the judging criteria does not take the number of likes you have into account.

10. I have included the necessary hashtag in my submission, but my photo did not appear in the contest website’s Gallery. Why?

It usually takes 1 to 2 hours for the admin to post submitted photos on the gallery. Please check again 2 hours after your submission and ensure the hashtag is correct.

11. I STILL do not see my post reflected in the contest website’s Gallery 2 hours after my submission. What should I do?

If you still do not see your post reflected in the Gallery after 2 hours, we strongly suggest for you to submit via Twitter or Instagram instead.
